Grains

Wheat

As wheat require a temperature about 21 - 24 deg , it is mostly grown in northern Indian states.Punbaj beign the highest producer.Since Southern India its too hot for it to grow the demand is high .

Rice

Rice crop requires temperature around 25°C along with heavy rainfall more than 100cm. So major producer are northern states like UP, punjab, Bihar and west Bengal. Scarcity in western dry regions like Gujarat and Rajasthan .

Pulses

Pulses require temperate climate and temperature around 18 to 30°C. Moderate rainfall of around 25-75cm Major producer UP, madhya pradesh Scarcity in J&K, Uttarakhand.

Sugarcane

ropical climate, temp. of 20-26° C Rainfall: average 150cm Producers: U.P., Odisha, Maharashtra Scarcity: Rajasthan, Kerala Chhattisgarh.

Millets

warm dry climate temp of 25-30°C Less rainfall of 45cm Major producer dry states like Rajasthan Scarcity in eastern side of India like Bihar, West Bengal, Andhra pradesh .

Maize

Maize require temperature about 21 to 27°C Rainfall 50-100 cm Major producer MP, Karnataka, Maharashtra Scarcity : kerela, Uttarakhand, and many northern Eastern Statesr..

Banana

Hot and humid conditions, temp. of 20°-27° C Ranifall: 650mm-750mm Major producer: Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Maharashtra Scarcity: J&K, Arunachal Pradesh, Uttrakhand, Himachal Pradesh.

Apple

Cold climates, temp. of -1°-24°C Rainfall: 1000-1250mm Altitude: 1500-2700m Major Producer: Arunachal Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, J&K Scarcity: (Most of India) Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Rajasthan.
